Ready to find meaningful connections? Here's how to get started as a dater:

  1. Download the app and create your account with a valid email address.
  2. Build your profile by adding photos, writing a bio, and sharing your interests and values.
  3. Complete the compatibility quiz to help us understand what you're looking for in a partner.
  4. Invite a trusted friend to be your matchmaker—they'll see potential matches and recommend people they think are right for you.
  5. Review your matches as they come in from your matchmaker, and start conversations with the ones who resonate.

Your matchmaker adds a personal touch to online dating, helping you find people who truly fit your personality and goals.

Want to help a friend find love? Here's how to become their matchmaker:

  1. Accept the invitation from your friend who wants you to be their matchmaker.
  2. Download the app and create your matchmaker account.
  3. Review your friend's profile to understand what they're looking for and confirm you know them well.
  4. Browse potential matches and use your knowledge of your friend to find people who would be a great fit.
  5. Send recommendations to your friend with a personal note about why you think each person could be a good match.

As a matchmaker, you bring the insight that algorithms can't—you know your friend's personality, sense of humor, and what makes them happy.
