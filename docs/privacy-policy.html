Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 1, 2026

At matchmate, we are committed to protecting your privacy and safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our dating application.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account information (name, email address, password)
  • Profile information (photos, bio, preferences, interests)
  • Quiz and compatibility test responses
  • Messages and communications with other users
  • Location data (if you choose to enable location services)

We may also automatically collect certain technical information such as device type, operating system, and app usage data to improve our services.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Match you with compatible users
  • Enable communication between users
  • Improve our matching features and user experience
  • Send you notifications and service-related updates
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and unsafe behavior
  • Comply with legal obligations

Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal information based on one or more of the following legal grounds:

  • Your consent
  • The performance of a contract (providing the app's services)
  • Our legitimate interests in operating and improving the app
  • Compliance with legal obligations

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information:

  • With other users as part of the matching and messaging features (profile information only)
  • With service providers that help us operate the app (such as hosting, analytics, and notification services)
  • When required by law or to protect the rights, safety, and security of matchmate or its users

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as necessary to provide our services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. However, no system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications
  • Request a copy of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

Tracking and Analytics

We may use analytics tools and similar technologies to understand how users interact with our app and to improve our services. These tools may collect device identifiers and usage data rather than traditional website cookies.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be stored and processed in countries outside your own. We take reasonable steps to ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data.

Children's Privacy

matchmate is intended for users who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy on this page and update the "Last Updated" date.
